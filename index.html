About me

I'm entering into my final year studying Electronic and Information Engineering at Imperial College London.

I'm interested in machine learning, signal processing and I'm hoping to learn more about FPGAs this year.

I love music and art and I enjoy tennis and running.

I hope to find a career where I can use creativity and electrons to solve problems and/or help society.
